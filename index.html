競賽說明

  • 10/4 00:00 開放線上註冊
  • 預賽為 48 小時的線上 Jeopardy 形式
  • 競賽採取積分累計制，比賽結束後依分數高低判定優勝，提供決賽資格予優勝者。同分者，依最後一次正確提交的時間判定
  • 比賽前十名隊伍，將晉級 12 月於台北舉行的 HITCON CTF Final (現場 Attack & Defense, 每隊 4 人)
  • 主辦單位將 "部分" 補助前十名隊伍參加決賽所需之交通及住宿費用
  • 2016 HITCON CTF 資格賽：
    下列競賽之冠軍隊伍將自動晉級決賽，不需參與 10/8 舉辦之預賽
    • TrendMicro CTF 2016
    • AIS3 CTF 2016
    • Smart Grid Hacking Competition
  • Flag 形式為: hitcon{printable ascii+}
  • 出題關主與工作人員均不列入成績計算

Contest Information

  • Online registration: 10/4 00:00 (UTC+8). The registration link is available from 10/4.
  • Qualification is online jeopardy for 48 hours.
  • Solving each problem will get certain points. Winners will be ranked by points.If the points are the same, the winners will be ranked by time of the last correct submission.
  • Top 10 teams will be invited to HITCON CTF Final on Dec 2-3 (Onsite Attack & Defense in Taipei, 4 players / team)
  • We will "partially" support top 10 teams for their flight and accommodation.
  • Currently 2016 HITCON CTF Qualifying Contests:
    The champions of the following contests will pre-qualify directly for the final in December and do not need to participate in the preliminary in 8th October.
    • TrendMicro CTF 2016
    • AIS3 CTF 2016
    • Smart Grid Hacking Competition
  • Flag format : hitcon{printable ascii+}
  • Game makers and staffs will not be counted in the ranking process.

競賽時間

  • 預賽: 10/8(六) 10:00 ~ 10/10(一) 10:00 (48HR)
  • 決賽: 12/2(五) ~ 12/3(六)

Contest time

  • Quals: Oct 8 10:00 AM ~ Oct 10 10:00 AM (GMT+8, 48 hours)
  • Final: Dec 2-3, 2016

  • 第一名獎金: $10,000 USD
  • 第二名獎金: $5,000 USD
  • 第三名獎金: $2,000 USD
  • 臺灣隊伍特殊獎: $1,000 USD

Awards in final

  • 1st place: $10,000 USD
  • 2nd place: $5,000 USD
  • 3rd place: $2,000 USD
  • Special award for Top 1 Taiwan team: $1,000 USD

比賽注意事項

競賽期間，做出以下違規事項者將直接取消資格：

  • 進行任何破壞競賽公平公正原則之行為
  • 散播、與其它隊伍交換關卡金鑰
  • 除競賽中提供的關卡外，禁止對競賽系統、參賽者及其它人員進行攻擊

每位參賽者最多僅能參與一個隊伍

Contest rules

During the contest, any of the following behaviors will lead to disqualification:

  • Any behavior that compromises the fairness of contest
  • Publishing, exchanging problem solutions
  • Attacking any personnel, including participants and non-participants, or the contest system itself

Each player can only join one team
